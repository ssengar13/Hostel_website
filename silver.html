6 Sharing Room with (Common Bathroom)

6 Sleeps 6 beds

The Silver 6 Sharing room offers a comfortable and budget-friendly option for residents. This room is designed to accommodate six individuals, each provided with their own bed, almirah, and study table for personal convenience. The common bathroom is located just outside the dorm, maintained regularly for hygiene and cleanliness. It’s an ideal choice for those looking for a communal living experience without compromising on essential amenities.

With 24/7 high-speed WiFi, security through CCTV surveillance, and easy access to Kerala-style meals served three times a day, you’ll have everything you need for a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

Room facilities

Large bed Washing Machine High speed WiFi
Indoor Games CCTV 24/7 support
Storage of luggage Cloud kitchen
